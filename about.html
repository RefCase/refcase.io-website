The Full Story

A simple frustration
became a product

RefCase started with a simple frustration: sales reps waste too much time digging through CRM reports just to find a good customer reference. As AEs ourselves, we've felt that pain — the delays, the back-and-forth, and the missed opportunity to build trust with a prospect at the right moment.

"We built RefCase to fix that."

Our goal is to make customer stories instantly accessible. No more hunting through Salesforce, no more outdated spreadsheets, no more improvising customer stories or guessing. Just fast, accurate reference data exactly when you need it — so you can focus on selling, not searching.

RefCase is built for modern sales teams who value speed, credibility, and clean workflows. Whether you're running discovery, preparing a pitch, or trying to close a deal, we want to give you the confidence of having the right story at your fingertips.

What we stand for
