The Problem

Every Deal has This Moment...

You are in a call and the prospect asks:

"Do you work with companies like us?"

Most reps stutter and reply something like:

"Uhh, I will check and get back to you"

...or they mention the same few generic reference cases.

This Moment Exposes Three Problems Every Sales Team Has...

01
Knowledge Gap

Your best stories are trapped in people's heads

Why customers bought, what they replaced, what actually tipped the decision — that knowledge lives in CRM fields nobody reads and conversations nobody recorded. In a live call, your rep can't access any of it. So they fall back on the same two overused stories, or worse, they wing it.

02
Credibility Gap

Bad answer kills momentum instantly

There's no recovering from a hesitant "I'll check and follow up." By the time proof arrives in a follow-up email, the moment is gone. Credibility dropped. Doubt crept in. The deal is now harder to close than it had to be.

03
Growth Gap

New reps start blind and prospects can tell

BDRs and new AEs have never seen most of your customer wins. They ramp slowly, reference poorly, and prospects can tell. Every quarter a new hire spends guessing is pipeline quietly bleeding out.

This is how most sales teams find customer proof today.
