Features

Everything you need to manage references at scale

Stop relying on phone tag and manual follow-ups. RefCase gives your team a single workspace to collect, track, and report on professional references.

Automated Requests

Send professional reference request emails in one click. Customizable templates adapt to any role, seniority level, or industry automatically.

Real-Time Tracking

Know exactly when your reference requests are opened, started, and submitted. Automated reminders reduce follow-up time by 78%.

Smart Questionnaires

Pre-built and fully customizable questionnaire templates. Ask the right questions for technical roles, leadership positions, and everything between.

Insight Reports

Generate shareable, beautifully formatted reference reports. Synthesized insights help hiring managers make confident, informed decisions faster.

How It Works

From request to report in 3 steps

RefCase handles the entire reference workflow so your team spends less time coordinating and more time placing great talent.

1

Create a case

Add a candidate, select references, and choose your questionnaire template. RefCase handles formatting and sends professional requests automatically.

2

Track responses

Monitor submission status in real time. Automated reminders nudge outstanding references, so you never have to chase manually again.

3

Share the report

Download or share a polished reference report with your hiring team. Synthesized scores and verbatim responses — all in one place.
